# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all Chrome extensions published by 
CodeSlayer Austin Rejepov under the developer account austin.rejepov+github@gmail.com

## Data Collection
Our extensions do not collect, store, or transmit any personal user 
data to external servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## Data Storage
Any data saved by our extensions (such as scan results or preferences) 
is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs. It never 
leaves your device.

## Permissions
Permissions used by our extensions are limited to what is strictly 
necessary for core functionality. No permission is used to collect 
personal data.

## Third Parties
We do not sell, trade, or transfer user data to any third parties.
We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.
